Rosemary Garlic Panfried Steak

Description

Delicious pan seared steak, oven finished with garlic rosemary butter for those smokey flavors usually reserved for the barbeque pit.

  • 1 T-bone steak:roughly 1 to 1-1/2"
  • 1-2 sprigs of fresh rosemary:chopped
  • 1-2 sprigs of fresh thyme:chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1-2 whole garlic cloves
  • Salt & pepper:to taste
